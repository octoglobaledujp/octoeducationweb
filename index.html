Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for Parents

Q. Is zero Japanese OK?
Yes. Our Japanese Language Program starts from absolute zero. Full English and Burmese support is available throughout the entire process.
Q. When and how do I pay?
Payment details are explained clearly at service start. Installment plans are available — just ask us.
Q. Can I join consultations as a parent?
Absolutely. We highly encourage parents to join. We speak Burmese, English, and Japanese — whichever you prefer.
Q. What if my child struggles after arrival?
Monthly follow-ups are fully included. We are available for academic, lifestyle, and emotional support questions.
Q. Which universities can my child apply to?
APU (Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University), Seinan Gakuin, Fukuoka University, and many more across Japan. We match each student to their absolute best fit.
Q. Is admission guaranteed?
We cannot guarantee acceptance, but we maximize every single chance with the strongest application files and best-matched choices.
